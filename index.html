Service we provide Our Services

Tree Felling

Felling is a process of cutting down trees from the floor. This is the safest way to take down a tree.

Tree Trimming / Lopping

This involves taking off small parts of a tree branches to provide shape and take away density from the tree canopy.

Pollarding

A pruning system involving the upper branches of the tree. Cutting from the top but leaving the main stem of the tree to promote growth.

Dismantling

Taking off certain sections of the tree that are hazardous to the public due to failure of a branch or disease in the tree.

Stump Grinding

Stump grinding is removing a tree stump that has been cut to ground level (does not remove roots but stops tree growing back).
